# From Sales to Platform

> A shift pattern where a company moves from selling products or services one transaction at a time to orchestrating a platform where third parties, developers, partners, or users create compounding value.

# From Sales to Platform

Use when the company shifts from selling its own product/service to enabling other participants to create value on a shared platform.

Signals:

- Multi-sided customer segments emerge.
- APIs, marketplaces, developer tools, or app ecosystems become key channels.
- Key Activities include governance, enablement, and trust.
- Revenue depends on ecosystem participation, not only direct sales.

Do not use for a normal reseller channel. The story must show platform economics or ecosystem leverage.
