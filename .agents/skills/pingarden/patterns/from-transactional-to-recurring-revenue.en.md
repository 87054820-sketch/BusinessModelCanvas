# From Transactional to Recurring Revenue

Use when the case shifts revenue logic from one-off sales to subscriptions, replenishment, recurring contracts, or continuous usage.

Signals:

- Revenue Streams become recurring.
- Customer Relationships emphasize retention and ongoing value.
- Key Activities include service reliability, personalization, or churn reduction.
- Unit economics depend on lifetime value, not only gross margin per sale.

Do not use for ordinary repeat purchases unless the business model deliberately designs recurrence.
