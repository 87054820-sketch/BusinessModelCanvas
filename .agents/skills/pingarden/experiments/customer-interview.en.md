# Customer Interview

> 30-60 minute conversation with target customers about their world, jobs, pains, and gains. The first move when you have a desirability hypothesis but no data — surfaces the problem framing, not the solution.

## Slug

`customer-interview` — referenced by AI agents matching a riskiest assumption to a candidate experiment. Cross-reference into canvases is via `experiment.appliesToCanvases[]`.

# Customer Interview — TL;DR for AI agents

**When to recommend.** User has a Desirability hypothesis with zero data, OR is starting a VPC / Empathy Map / JTBD with the customer side empty, OR landed on a "riskiest assumption" sticky and asks "how do I test this?".

**Strength of evidence.** Weak. Discovers the problem frame; does NOT validate willingness to pay or buying intent. If the user pushes for "is this validated?" — say no, recommend Smoke Test / Concierge / Letter of Intent next.

**Sample size to claim a pattern.** N=5 floor, N=8-12 sweet spot. Below 5 don't claim a pattern; below 12 don't claim "we've covered the segment." Themes count as "validated for further testing" when 3 of 5 surface them unprompted.

**Setup.** Hours: a discussion guide (5-7 themes, 1 opener + 2-3 probes each), recruiting message, 30-60 min slots.

**Run.** Hours per interview, days for the round.

**Capabilities the team needs.** `interview-design` (open-ended, non-leading), `active-listening` (probe specifics, not opinions), `note-taking` (verbatim quotes, not paraphrase).

**Decision tree.**

- Started with this canvas and the right column is empty? → Customer Interview, then come back and fill the canvas.
- Already did 5+ interviews? → Move to Smoke Test (Desirability + quantitative) or Concierge (Desirability + Viability) — DON'T do another interview round.
- Hypothesis is about pricing or willingness to pay? → Don't recommend Customer Interview. Recommend Pre-Sale / Letter of Intent.
- Can't access the segment? → Suggest Discussion Forums / Search Trend Analysis as passive Discovery substitutes.

**Anti-patterns to flag.**

- Pitching solution mid-interview ("would you use this?") collects compliments, not data.
- Friends and existing customers ≠ early adopters of a new direction.
- Calling N=5 interviews "validated" — they Discover, not Validate.

**Cross-canvas mapping.** Outputs land on `value-proposition-canvas` (Jobs / Pains / Gains right column), `empathy-map` (Says / Thinks / Feels / Does), `jobs-to-be-done` (full functional / emotional / social columns), `customer-journey` (steps + emotional curve). When the user is editing one of these, surface this experiment first.
