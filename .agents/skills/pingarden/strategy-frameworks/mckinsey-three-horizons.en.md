# McKinsey Three Horizons

## Select this framework when

- The user has multiple businesses or innovation bets across different maturity levels.
- The key issue is growth sequencing: core, emerging, future.
- The user asks how to fund the future without starving the present.

## Use these canvases

1. `three-horizons-map` — place H1, H2, H3, migration actions, evidence risks.
2. `portfolio-map` — compare risk/return and Explore/Exploit position.
3. `experiment-canvas` and `evidence-scorecard` — validate H2/H3 assumptions.
4. `business-model-canvas` — expand serious bets into full model logic.

## Questions to ask

- What is the unit of analysis?
- Which H1 businesses fund the company today?
- Which H2 bets have real evidence?
- Which H3 options are learning bets rather than slogans?
- What evidence allows movement between horizons?

## Red flags

Do not treat horizons as calendar years. Do not call untested ideas H2. Do not let H1 financial metrics kill every H2/H3 option.
