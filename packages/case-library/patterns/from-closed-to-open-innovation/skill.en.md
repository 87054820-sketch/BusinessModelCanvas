# From Closed to Open Innovation

Use when a case shifts from closed internal R&D to outside-in or inside-out innovation.

Signals:

- External ideas, IP, startups, universities, or platforms enter the model.
- Internal assets are licensed, pooled, spun out, or commercialized by others.
- BMC changes appear in Key Partners, Key Resources, Key Activities, Cost Structure, and Revenue Streams.

Do not use for any company that merely has partners. The story must explain the innovation boundary shift.
