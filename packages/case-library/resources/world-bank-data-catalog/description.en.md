# Reading map — World Bank Data Catalog

World Bank Data Catalog is the broadest starting point for macro and development data in PinGarden. Use it when a strategy claim depends on country context, development indicators, public finance, energy, infrastructure, or long-run environment signals.

## What it helps answer

- Is the target market growing in the macro sense?
- Which country or region indicators support an environment scan?
- What external signals should enter a Scenario Matrix?
- Which development or infrastructure constraints affect the BMC?

## How to use with canvases

- `business-model-environment`: convert indicators into trend, market, industry, or macro-force stickies.
- `scenario-matrix`: use changes in indicators as early signals or uncertainty inputs.
- `business-model-canvas`: attach data to assumptions about customers, channels, partners, costs, and revenue.

## Limitations

World Bank indicators are often country-level and lagged. They may not describe a niche segment, firm-level behavior, or fast-moving technology adoption. Always record indicator name, geography, year, and whether the data is a direct measure or proxy.

## Evidence discipline

A macro indicator supports a hypothesis; it does not prove that a strategy will work. Pair it with customer, competitive, and business-model evidence.
