# Source Notes — Business Model Portfolio Management

Source: Alex Osterwalder, Yves Pigneur, Fred Etiemble, Alan Smith, *The Invincible Company*, Wiley, 2020.

## Extracted method spine

- **Business Model Portfolio**: the collection of existing business models a company exploits and new business models it explores to avoid disruption and ensure longevity.
- **Portfolio Map**: a strategic management tool to visualize, analyze, and manage both improving/growing business models and future business models being searched/tested.
- **Portfolio dichotomy**:
  - `Explore`: innovation projects, new business models, new value propositions, products, and services.
  - `Exploit`: existing businesses, value propositions, products, and services.
- **Three management activities**: `Visualize → Analyze → Manage`.
- **Explore axes**: Expected Return × Innovation Risk.
- **Exploit axes**: Return × Death & Disruption Risk.
- **Explore actions**: Ideate, Persevere, Pivot, Retire, Spinout, Transfer, Invest.
- **Exploit actions**: Acquire, Merge, Partner, Invest, Improve, Divest, Dismantle.
