# Pre-Sale — TL;DR for AI agents

**When to recommend.** B2C / prosumer with single sticker price ($20-$500). Smoke Test passed; next gate is "will signups actually pay?". User can deliver in the promised timeframe.

**Strength of evidence.** Strong. Real money filters out polite intent — biggest behavior change vs verbal claims.

**Sample.** 1000-5000 targeted visits, conversion threshold + revenue threshold both pre-set.

**Setup / run.** Weeks (page + payment + ad creative + delivery plan) / weeks.

**Capabilities.** `marketing-launch`, `payment-processing`, `customer-communication`.

**Decision tree.**

- Can't deliver in promised timeframe? → DON'T recommend. Reputation napalm.
- B2B with procurement? → DON'T recommend. Letter of Intent.
- Enterprise sales pricing (no sticker)? → DON'T recommend. Letter of Intent.
- Regulated category (medical/financial)? → Push back; legal review needed first.

**Anti-patterns.**

- Promise more than you can ship.
- No refund policy.
- Untargeted traffic = curiosity refunds.
- Treating revenue as profit (it's working capital with a delivery promise).

**Cross-canvas.** Validates `business-model-canvas` (revenue streams + customer segments at the price point), `value-proposition-canvas` (which value crystallised into actual payment).
