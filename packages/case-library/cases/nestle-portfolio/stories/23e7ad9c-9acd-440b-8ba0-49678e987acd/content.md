# Nestlé exploit portfolio actions

Nestlé is useful for reading the Exploit side of the Portfolio Map. The important lesson is that mature categories still require active management. A portfolio can be improved through acquire, partner, improve, divest, and dismantle decisions.

## Portfolio reading

The map separates large current categories from portfolio actions. Beverages and pet care are existing engines; licensing, acquisitions, and plant-based bets are moves around the edge of the current portfolio. Strategic reviews and divestments show that exploit management is not just growth, but also pruning.

## Transfer lesson

For incumbents, portfolio discipline means asking whether every business still earns its place. Improving and divesting can be as strategic as launching something new.
