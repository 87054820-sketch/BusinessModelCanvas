# Bosch Accelerator explore portfolio journey

Bosch is useful for reading the Explore side of the Portfolio Map. Exploration is not a single big bet; it is a managed funnel where many ideas enter, evidence reduces uncertainty, and only a few ideas deserve transfer into the existing business portfolio.

## Portfolio reading

The map deliberately shows many teams starting in high-risk territory. The portfolio becomes healthier when weak ideas are retired early, promising teams persevere with stronger experiments, and a few de-risked models transfer toward exploitation.

## Transfer lesson

Explore portfolio management should make failure cheap and learning visible. The goal is not to keep every idea alive, but to produce a few credible future growth engines without betting the company too early.
