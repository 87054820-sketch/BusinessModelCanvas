# When to use the Innovation Culture Map

Use this canvas when innovation work is not failing because teams lack ideas, but because the organization keeps producing the wrong behaviors. It turns "culture" into an observable chain: current outcomes come from repeated behaviors, and repeated behaviors are shaped by enablers and blockers such as metrics, funding, governance, rituals, skills, and leadership signals.

The point is not to write values like "be bold" or "embrace failure." The point is to identify which conditions make exploration, experimentation, transfer, and scaling possible or impossible.

## Use it when

- Explore projects exist, but few become validated options or transfer into the core.
- Experiment canvases are created, but teams do not actually run tests or retire weak ideas.
- Leaders say they want innovation, while review meetings, budgets, or incentives reward only predictable execution.
- You need to redesign the mechanisms around behavior, not just ask people to "be more innovative."

## Do not use it when

- The problem is a single product decision, customer segment, or value proposition; use VPC, BMC, or Experiment Canvas first.
- You only want a morale exercise or culture slogan.
- Leadership is not willing to change funding, metrics, governance, or review behavior.

## Concepts to know first

**Outcomes** are what the current culture repeatedly produces. **Behaviors** are observable actions in meetings, reviews, projects, and decisions. **Enablers and blockers** are the formal and informal mechanisms that make those behaviors rational. Good culture work starts with evidence, not personality judgment.

## First step

Start with current behaviors. Write what people repeatedly do in a specific situation: who does what, where, and what signal it creates. Then connect those behaviors to current outcomes and the enablers/blockers that keep them repeating.

## What you should produce

The output should be a short change agenda: which behaviors to preserve or stop, which desired behaviors to build, which blockers to remove, which enablers to create, and which leadership actions are required in the next month or quarter.

## Next step

Use `portfolio-map` to see whether culture changes improve Explore/Exploit movement. Use `experiment-canvas` and `evidence-scorecard` to make desired innovation behaviors concrete and measurable.
