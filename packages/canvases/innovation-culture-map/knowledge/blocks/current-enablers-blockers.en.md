# Current enablers / blockers

Look for the mechanisms that cause current behaviors. The book stresses that enablers and blockers can be formal or informal.

Formal mechanisms: processes, budgets, KPIs, approvals, organization structure, rewards, legal or compliance gates.

Informal mechanisms: leadership preferences, meeting rituals, defaults, fear, knowledge gaps, and trust between teams.

Writing tips:
- Use green stickies for enablers and red stickies for blockers.
- Do not stop at “people are unwilling.” Ask what mechanism makes them act that way.
- Leadership behaviors can also be enablers or blockers.
