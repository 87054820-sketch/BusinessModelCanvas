# Key Trends

![Key Trends — Strategyzer detail page](assets/key-trends-detail.en.png)

The forces of change you can't control but must anticipate.
Strategyzer's "Foresight" lens — long-fuse shifts that reshape what's
possible, what's allowed, what's accepted, and what's affordable
inside any market.

## Sub-categories

### Technology Trends

> *Identifies technology trends that could threaten your business
> model — or enable it to evolve or improve.*

**Main Qs.** What are the major technology trends both inside and
outside your market? Which technologies represent important
opportunities or disruptive threats? Which emerging technologies are
peripheral customers adopting?

### Regulatory Trends

> *Describes regulations and regulatory trends that influence your
> business model.*

**Main Qs.** Which regulatory trends influence your market? What
rules may affect your business model? Which regulations and taxes
affect customer demand?

### Societal and Cultural Trends

> *Identifies major societal trends that may influence your business
> model.*

**Main Qs.** Describe key societal trends. Which shifts in cultural
or societal values affect your business model? Which trends might
influence buyer behavior?

### Socioeconomic Trends

> *Outlines major socioeconomic trends relevant to your business
> model.*

**Main Qs.** What are the key demographic trends? How would you
characterize income and wealth distribution in your market? How high
are disposable incomes? Describe spending patterns in your market
(e.g. housing, healthcare, entertainment, etc.). What portion of the
population lives in urban areas as opposed to rural settings?

## Drop-a-sticky examples

- **Technology** — "Generative AI commoditising basic content
  production at ~50% lower cost than 18 months ago"
- **Regulatory** — "Tightening data-residency regulation in the EU
  forcing on-soil hosting by Q3"
- **Societal & cultural** — "Gen Z preferring subscription over
  ownership across SaaS, music, and even cars"
- **Socioeconomic** — "Disposable income for our target segment up
  4% YoY despite headline inflation"

## How this feeds the BMC

Trends feed almost every BMC block, but most directly:
**Customer Segments** (who's emerging, who's shrinking),
**Value Propositions** (what customers will accept tomorrow),
**Channels** (which surfaces customers will be on), and
**Cost Structure** (which inputs are getting cheaper or more expensive).
A trend that doesn't point at any of these is decoration.
