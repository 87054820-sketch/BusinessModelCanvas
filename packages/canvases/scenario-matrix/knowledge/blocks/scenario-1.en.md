# Scenario 1

Each scenario should be a plausible world, not a label. Write a short story that explains market structure, customer behavior, competitor moves, channel changes, partner constraints, revenue logic, and cost pressure under this combination of the two axes.

## Good Answer vs Weak Answer

A good answer changes at least one strategic choice. A weak answer is only a mood, such as "optimistic future," or a list of disconnected trends.

## Common Pitfalls

- Making the first scenario the preferred future.
- Ignoring how the two uncertainties interact.
- Forgetting to pressure-test current BMC assumptions.

## Sticky Examples

- "Open regulation + fragmented platforms: customers demand portability; channel partnerships multiply."
- "AI budgets grow + proprietary models scarce: premium integration services become defensible."

## Transfer Questions

- Which value proposition gets stronger in this future?
- Which cost or partner assumption becomes fragile?
