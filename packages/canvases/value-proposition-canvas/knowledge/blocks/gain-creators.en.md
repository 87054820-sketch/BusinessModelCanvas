# Gain Creators (收益创造方案)

Gain Creators describe how your products and services create customer gains. They explicitly outline the expected or unexpected outcomes and benefits that you intend to offer your customers. This includes functional utility, cost savings, and positive emotions.

Because Pain Relievers are already in place, Gain Creators don't need to address every gain identified in the customer profile. Focus on the gains that are relevant and that differentiate your products and services.

## How Gain Creators Create Value

### Save Time, Money, or Effort

> *Does your product or service delight customers with savings? In terms of time, money, or effort spent?*

### Exceed Expectations

> *Does your product or service produce outcomes that go beyond what customers expect? By providing more or less to achieve a quality level?*

### Outperform Current Solutions

> *Does your product or service outperform current value propositions and make customers happy? In terms of specific features, performance, or quality?*

### Make Life Easier

> *Does your product or service make your customers' jobs or lives easier? More practical, easier to access, more services, lower acquisition costs?*

### Create Positive Social Impact

> *Does your product or service create positive social impact? By making them look better or increasing their power and prestige?*

### Do Something Special

> *Is your product or service doing something special that customers are looking for? In terms of product design, guarantees, or other features?*

### Fulfill Dreams

> *Does your product or service fulfill customer dreams? By helping them achieve aspirations or find relief from hardship?*

### Deliver Positive Results

> *Does your product or service produce positive outcomes that align with customer success and failure metrics? In terms of performance or reduced cost?*

### Make Adoption Easier

> *Does your product or service make adoption easier? Through lower cost, less investment, lower risk, better quality, more features, or better design?*

## Core Questions

- Which customer gains are we creating?
- How are we producing outcomes that exceed expectations?
- Are we focusing on the gains that differentiate us?
- Which gain creators are essential versus nice-to-have?

## Examples

- **Exceed expectations** ——「AI writing assistant that drafts first version 5x faster than manual writing」
- **Make life easier** ——「One-click integration with 50+ tools in your existing stack」
- **Fulfill dreams** ——「Personalized learning path that helps career switchers land a job in 90 days」
- **Do something special** ——「Lifetime access with free upgrades, no subscription fees ever」

## How it flows into VPC

Gain Creators answer the question: "How do we make the customer's life better?" They must directly address the most relevant gains identified in the customer profile. **A Gain Creator that doesn't map to a real customer gain is just marketing fluff.**
