# When to use the Value Proposition Canvas

The Value Proposition Canvas (VPC) zooms into the middle block of the
Business Model Canvas. It's a tool for testing **fit between a customer
segment and what you're offering them** — before you commit to building.

## Reach for it when

- You have a hunch about a customer segment but want to make the fit
  argument explicit.
- You're trying to articulate *why* a feature should exist, not just
  *what* it does.
- A team is rallying around an idea but hasn't named the underlying
  customer job clearly.
- You're preparing customer-discovery interviews and need a
  hypothesis-shaped target.

## Skip it when

- You don't yet have a candidate customer segment — start broader.
- You're modelling the whole business — use the Business Model Canvas.
- You're optimising an existing, well-understood product feature —
  the VPC's discovery framing will feel heavyweight.
