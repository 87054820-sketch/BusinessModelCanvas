# Technology Development

R&D, process technology, product design, software platforms, automation, and data infrastructure. The question: where does technology compound advantage, and where is it just table stakes the whole industry has?

## Examples

- "Proprietary ML platform for personalisation; 5-year head start on incumbents."
- "Shared electronic-waybill standard for third-party courier partners."
- "Closed-loop materials science R&D producing 2 new fibres per year."

## Flow

Distinguish between *technology that compounds* (proprietary, data-effect, network-effect) and *technology that levels the field* (CRM, ERP, cloud infra). For platform / SaaS businesses, this zone is usually the load-bearing activity — Inbound and Outbound Logistics shrink, Tech Development expands. Make the distinction explicit before discussing budget.
