# Marketing & Sales

Demand generation, brand building, channels, pricing, and the sales force. The question: how does the firm reach the customer and convert demand into revenue at a cost that fits the unit economics?

## Examples

- "Brand-led D2C with paid + organic mix; CAC ≤ 30% of first-year LTV."
- "Enterprise outbound + customer success motion; 12-month sales cycle."
- "Marketplace SEO + creator partnerships; revenue share with named affiliates."

## Flow

CAC per channel and LTV per segment go to the BMC Revenue Streams + Cost Structure. Brand and channel commitments go to Key Resources / Channels. If Marketing promises something Operations or Service cannot deliver, that is a *linkage leak* — flag it in purple between blocks.
