# Outbound Logistics

The storage, fulfilment, and delivery of finished product to the customer. The question: how does the firm get the right product to the right place at the right time and cost, with the right post-sale information flow back?

## Examples

- "Cross-dock distribution network with regional same-day fulfilment."
- "Direct-to-consumer shipping including reverse logistics for 30-day returns."
- "API-led delivery with real-time tracking pushed to customer + merchant."

## Flow

For e-commerce / physical-goods businesses, outbound is often the cost crucible. For SaaS / digital-only businesses, this zone shrinks to near-zero — document why and move the analysis weight to Technology Development. If outbound is a differentiator (e.g., 2h delivery), it belongs in the Value Proposition.
