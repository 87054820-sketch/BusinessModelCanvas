# When to use the Experiment Canvas

The Experiment Canvas turns risky assumptions into observable, measurable, reviewable experiments. Use it when you already have a value proposition, business-model direction, or design criteria, but still need evidence for the assumptions that matter most.

## Reach for it when

- You have identified the most dangerous uncertainty.
- The team wants to learn before investing significant build or operating effort.
- You need to define method, metrics, sample, timeframe, and ownership.
- You want interviews, landing pages, prototype tests, A/B tests, or sales trials to become a repeatable learning loop.

## Skip it when

- You do not yet have a clear problem or assumption to test.
- You are planning delivery work rather than validating uncertainty.
- You already have sufficient evidence and only need execution.
