# Experiment Setup

Define how the experiment will run: method, prototype material, sample, timeframe, owners, and variables. This area also borrows from Open Practice Library's People, Trials, and Scientists lenses.

## Possible methods

- Customer interviews or concept tests
- Landing-page or ad-click tests
- Clickable prototype / Concierge MVP
- A/B test or rollout experiment
- Sales script, pricing, or letter-of-intent test

## Check

- Who is the experiment subject, and who is affected?
- Is the sample representative enough?
- Who owns recruiting, running, recording, and reviewing the experiment?
