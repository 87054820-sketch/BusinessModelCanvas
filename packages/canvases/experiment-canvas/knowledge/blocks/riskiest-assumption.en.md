# Riskiest Assumption

Write the assumption that creates the most risk right now. It often comes from value proposition, business model, channels, pricing, customer demand, or organizational adoption.

## Check

- If this is wrong, does the idea fail?
- Is it earlier, cheaper, or more necessary to test than other assumptions?
- Is it specific, not just “users will like us”?

## Examples

- “Target customers will pay monthly for automated reports”
- “Procurement owners can finish security review in 7 days”
