# Results & Conclusion

After the experiment, record what actually happened: quantitative data, qualitative feedback, anomalies, and sample limits. Then decide whether the hypothesis is validated, invalidated, or inconclusive.

## Check

- Did the actual data meet the predefined success criteria?
- Is there evidence against the hypothesis?
- Is the data clear enough, or do you need more samples?
- Did external variables affect the result?

## Examples

- “32 bookings, 9.4% conversion — validated.”
- “Only 1 customer agreed to try it — invalidated.”
