# Falsifiable Hypothesis

Rewrite the risky assumption as a statement that data can validate or disprove. A useful formula: We believe “a specific action” will drive “a measurable outcome” within “a timeframe.”

## Check

- Is the action specific and executable?
- Is the result observable and measurable?
- Is the timeframe explicit?
- Is there an outcome that would make you admit the hypothesis is false?

## Examples

- “We believe fewer form fields will increase completion by 25% in two weeks.”
- “We believe at least 3 of 5 target customers will try the prototype.”
