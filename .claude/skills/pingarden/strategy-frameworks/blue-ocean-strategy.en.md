# Blue Ocean Strategy

> A strategic analysis framework for creating uncontested market space through value innovation: simultaneously increasing buyer value and reducing cost by redesigning the factors of competition.

## Slug

`blue-ocean-strategy` — referenced by `CaseLibraryEntry.appliesStrategyFrameworks[]` on cases that demonstrate this analysis method.

# Blue Ocean Strategy — AI guide

Use this framework when the user needs to compare a company against an industry playbook, design a differentiated value curve, or ask whether an idea can create uncontested market space.

## Fill order

1. Read the case or user idea and identify the **red-ocean industry norm**.
2. Define 5–10 **factors of competition** customers actually compare.
3. Plot incumbents and the focal company on the same factors using `blue-ocean-strategy-canvas`.
4. Derive ERRC from the curve: **Eliminate / Reduce / Raise / Create**.
5. Translate the new value curve into BMC implications: value proposition, segments, channels, key activities, resources, partners, revenue, and cost.

## Quality bar

- The blue-ocean curve must have a different shape, not just higher scores.
- Every score needs a source or explicit assumption.
- Explain the noncustomers or underserved buyers unlocked by the move.
- Pair every Strategy Canvas case with at least one BMC story.

## Anti-patterns

- Do not classify Blue Ocean Strategy as a business-model pattern.
- Do not tag a case as blue-ocean just because it is innovative.
- Do not write ERRC before plotting the competitive factors.
- Do not copy long book passages; write original summaries with citations.

## References

### Books

- **Kim & Mauborgne 2015** · *W. Chan Kim & Renée Mauborgne · Blue Ocean Strategy, Expanded Edition · Harvard Business Review Press* · 2015
  Canonical source for value innovation, Strategy Canvas, ERRC, noncustomers, and market-boundary reconstruction.

- **BMG 2010** · *Alexander Osterwalder & Yves Pigneur · Business Model Generation · Wiley* · 2010 · Strategy chapter: Business Model Perspective on Blue Ocean Strategy
  Connects Blue Ocean Strategy to the Business Model Canvas: value-side changes must be checked against cost, activities, resources, and partners.

### Web

- **BOS official site** · *[Blue Ocean Strategy · What is Blue Ocean Strategy](https://www.blueoceanstrategy.com/what-is-blue-ocean-strategy/)*
  Concise official definition: simultaneous pursuit of differentiation and low cost to create new demand and make competition irrelevant.

- **BOS examples** · *[Blue Ocean Strategy · 7 Powerful Blue Ocean Strategy Examples](https://www.blueoceanstrategy.com/blog/7-powerful-blue-ocean-strategy-examples/)*
  Public example pool used to prioritize Cirque du Soleil, Yellow Tail, Nintendo Wii, Marvel, Stitch Fix, HealthMedia, Nickel, and related market-creating moves.
