# Search Trend Analysis — TL;DR for AI agents

**When to recommend.** Interviews produced specific problem language; user wants to see if it scales on the public web. Or sizing two segment candidates relative to each other. Or sanity-checking a "key trend" in the BMC Environment.

**Strength of evidence.** Weak. Confirms vocabulary travels and trend direction; does NOT confirm willingness to pay or addressable market in dollars.

**Setup / run.** Hours / hours.

**Capabilities.** `keyword-research`, `trend-analysis`.

**Decision tree.**

- Niche is B2B with analyst-driven buying? → Don't recommend. Search signal weak. Suggest Customer Interview + Discussion Forums on industry-specific venues instead.
- Trend line falling over 5 years? → Surface this as a red flag — going to market against a falling trend is a known pitfall.
- High Google volume but no Reddit/HN discussion? → Likely SEO hijacked. Caveat the volume number.
- User wants market sizing in $? → DON'T recommend search trends alone. Pair with Letter of Intent or Pre-Sale for actual demand.

**Anti-patterns.**

- One head term ≠ picture. Need 5-15.
- 30-day spike ≠ trend. Use 5-year window.
- Volume ≠ TAM.

**Cross-canvas.** Validates `business-model-environment`'s key-trends block, sharpens `value-proposition-canvas` Pains by relative magnitude.
