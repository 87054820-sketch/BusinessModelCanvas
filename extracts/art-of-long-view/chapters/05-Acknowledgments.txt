# Acknowledgments

If this book is readable and useful it is due to the considerable
talents, persistence, and support of three people: Harriet Rubin, my
editor at Doubleday, whose consistent insights into the mind of the
reader gave this book its form and guided its evolution, and Howard
Rheingold and Art Kleiner, the two writers whose labors provided
words for my often inarticulate ideas.
My most important teacher, my toughest critic, and my strongest
supporter was my wife, Cathleen, who was remarkably patient
during the seemingly endless gestation of this modest work while
she had a briefer gestation of a far greater work, our son Benjamin.
