# Current outcomes

Outcomes are the concrete consequences of behavior. They can be positive or negative. Do not write targets; write what already happens or repeatedly happens.

Check questions:
- What does the current culture actually harvest?
- Which outcomes are produced by the behaviors you mapped?
- Are there outcomes with no matching behavior? If so, go back and add the missing behaviors.

Examples:
- Many ideas, little real customer evidence.
- Reliable core execution, but too few future growth options.
- Weak projects run too long, consuming resources without learning.
