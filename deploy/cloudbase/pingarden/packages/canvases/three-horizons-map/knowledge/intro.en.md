Three Horizons Map is a portfolio lens for growth over time. It separates what funds the company today, what can become the next growth engine, and what is still an early option.

Use it when the strategic question is not only “which bets are attractive?” but “how do these bets mature, receive evidence, and move between horizons?” Pair it with `portfolio-map` for risk/return position and with `experiment-canvas` / `evidence-scorecard` for de-risking H2 and H3 bets.
