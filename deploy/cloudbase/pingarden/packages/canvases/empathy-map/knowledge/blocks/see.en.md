# See

What's in their environment — the stimuli reaching them every day.
Physical surroundings, what's on their screen, what competitors are
doing, what they witness peers do, what trends are visible in their
world.

This is the **first quadrant of the outside circuit**. Stick to
observable phenomena: if you couldn't film it from across the room,
it doesn't go here — it goes in Think & Feel.

## Ask

- What do they look at first thing when they sit down to work?
- What's on their screen most of the day?
- Who is around them, and what are those people doing?
- What are competitors / peers shipping or saying that they notice?
- What trends, signals, or news are visibly reaching them?
- What does their physical workspace look like?

## Tip

The richest "See" stickies often come from a screen-share or a
ride-along, not from an interview question. People rarely describe
their own environment well — they're too inside it.
