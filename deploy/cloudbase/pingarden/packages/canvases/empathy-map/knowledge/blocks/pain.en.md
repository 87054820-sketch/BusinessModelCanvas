# Pains

Fears, frustrations, anxieties, obstacles. The blockers and negative
experiences shaping how the person feels about their situation right
now.

A well-formed Pain sticky names the **friction *and* the unresolved
cost**. "Spends 4 hours a week reconciling spreadsheets" beats "Data
is hard." The first is actionable; the second is vibes.

## Ask

- What's keeping them up at night?
- What do they fear losing — control, status, relationships, time,
  money, identity?
- What do they consider risky, costly, or just plain annoying?
- What obstacle has been there for months and shows no sign of moving?
- What do they wish they could ask for, but can't?
- What problem keeps coming back even after they think they've solved
  it?

## How this connects forward

Pains here feed directly into the **Pains** column of the Value
Proposition Canvas (see Pairs With). When you go to design the
offering, every "pain reliever" should be able to point at one of the
stickies in this quadrant. If a reliever doesn't, it's a feature
looking for a problem.
