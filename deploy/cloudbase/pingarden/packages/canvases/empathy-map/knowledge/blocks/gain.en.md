# Gains

Wants, needs, hopes, dreams, definitions of success. What "winning"
looks like — to the person, in their own terms, not yours.

Borrowing Strategyzer's vocabulary (helpful when this map feeds the
Value Proposition Canvas downstream), gains come in four flavours:

- **Required** — must-haves; without them they reject the option.
- **Expected** — standard expectations; tolerated but not celebrated.
- **Desired** — things they'd actively choose if offered.
- **Unexpected** — positive surprises beyond their imagination.

## Ask

- What does "good" look like for them this quarter / year?
- What would they brag about — to their boss, partner, next
  interviewer?
- What metric or moment would tell them this period was a success?
- What outcome would change how they feel about their work / life?
- What would they happily pay (in time, money, political capital) for?

## How this connects forward

Gains here feed directly into the **Gains** column of the Value
Proposition Canvas. Each "gain creator" you design later should point
at one of the stickies in this quadrant. If it doesn't, you're
producing a benefit nobody wished for.
