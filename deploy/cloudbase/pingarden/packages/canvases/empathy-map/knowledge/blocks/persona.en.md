# Goal / Persona

The person you are trying to deeply understand, and the situation
they're in. This zone anchors every other quadrant of the map to one
specific human in one specific moment. The map's job is to **reflect
them**, not to redesign them.

## What to capture

- **Who** — name + role + the relevant context (industry, life stage,
  tenure). Make them concrete enough that the team is talking about
  one human.
- **Situation** — the activity they're engaged in right now. "Trying
  to launch X by Y", "Onboarding to a new tool", "Recovering from a
  failed quarter."
- **Goal** — stated as a verb-first sentence in their voice, not
  yours. "Land the AI roadmap with engineering", "Find childcare that
  actually works", "Survive this quarter without burning out my team."

## Avoid

- **Demographic shopping lists.** "32, urban, two kids" tells you very
  little about how to help them. The situation and the goal carry the
  weight.
- **Your goal for them.** It's not "we want them to convert." It's
  what *they* are trying to do.
- **Trying to cover several segments in one persona.** If the goal
  diverges across segments, duplicate the canvas.
