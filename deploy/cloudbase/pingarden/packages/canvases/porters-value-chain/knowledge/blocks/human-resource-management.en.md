# Human Resource Management

Recruiting, training, compensation, culture, retention, and leadership development. The question: which capabilities live inside people, and where does the talent pipeline make or break the strategy?

## Examples

- "Specialist hiring + apprenticeship pipeline for senior engineers; 18-month internal promotion path."
- "Mission-driven culture with public ESG commitments; lowers attrition to half industry median."
- "Distributed engineering bench across 3 time zones; 24h handoff handbook."

## Flow

HRM is the most-overlooked source of moat. If the strategy is service-led, mission-led, or specialist-led, HRM is usually load-bearing. Map talent assumptions to BMC Key Resources (the people-side), and flag explicitly when an Ansoff or Three-Horizons move requires capabilities the firm does not yet have.
