# Margin

The difference between the value the customer pays and the total cost of every activity in the chain. The question: where does margin actually come from — cost discipline, differentiation premium, or both?

## Examples

- "Premium pricing supported by Service + brand; 38% gross margin on hero SKUs."
- "Cost leadership from Operations scale; 12% margin sustained by 3× competitor volume."
- "Mixed: brand premium on flagship line (45% GM), cost discipline on long-tail (15% GM)."

## Flow

Margin synthesises the whole chain. If margin is shrinking, walk back: is a cost-advantage activity eroding, is a differentiation activity being copied, or is a linkage leaking? The margin zone is also the truth-test for "stuck in the middle" — firms claiming both cost and differentiation often have neither in the margin numbers.
