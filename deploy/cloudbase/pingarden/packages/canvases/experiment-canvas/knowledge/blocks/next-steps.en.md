# Next Steps

Use the conclusion to decide whether to persevere, pivot, or redo the experiment. Avoid “keep watching”; define the action, owner, and timing.

## Three moves

- **Persevere**: the assumption is validated; expand sample, run the next experiment, or begin delivery.
- **Pivot**: the assumption is invalidated; change customer, value proposition, channel, pricing, or solution.
- **Redo**: the result is unclear; improve design, expand sample, or change measurement.

## Examples

- “Persevere: expand sample to 200 target customers, owned by growth lead.”
- “Pivot: test a lower-priced entry offer; landing page due next Friday.”
