# Strategy lenses: Three Horizons and McKinsey 7S

Bosch Accelerator is not just a list of innovation projects. Through the **Three Horizons** lens, the incumbent manufacturing base is H1, validated internal ventures are potential H2 growth engines, and early ideas are H3 options. The accelerator matters because it creates a migration system from option to evidence-backed venture.

::canvas[three-horizons-map]{}

The **McKinsey 7S** lens makes the organizational challenge visible. Strategy says Bosch wants future growth beyond the core. Structure gives internal teams a separate accelerator path. Systems provide evidence reviews and venture gates. Shared values must legitimize learning before scale. Skills include discovery, prototyping, and venture-building, which are different from core engineering execution. Style and staff determine whether sponsors protect learning or force early core-business metrics.

A strong accelerator changes the surrounding 7S system so evidence can move a venture toward H2 without either suffocating it with H1 controls or shielding it forever from commercial discipline.
