# Porter’s Five Forces

## Select this framework when

- The user needs to understand industry attractiveness, margin pressure, or competitive structure.
- The case involves entering, repositioning, or defending in a market.

## Use these canvases

1. `business-model-environment` — map forces as external pressure.
2. `business-model-canvas` — translate forces into model choices.
3. `design-criteria-canvas` — write rules for a defensible model.

## Questions to ask

- What is the industry boundary?
- Who are competitors, buyers, suppliers, entrants, and substitutes?
- Which force captures most profit pressure?
- Which BMC blocks must change in response?

## Red flags

Do not make it only a competitor list. Do not ignore substitutes. Do not assume market growth means attractiveness.
