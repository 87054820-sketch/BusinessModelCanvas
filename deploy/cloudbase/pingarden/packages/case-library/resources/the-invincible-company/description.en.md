# Reading map — The Invincible Company

Read this between **Portfolio Map** and **Innovation Metrics**. It does not treat canvases as isolated templates; it connects business-model portfolios, Explore/Exploit, innovation metrics, culture, and experiment cadence into one management system.

## What problem it solves

- Companies optimize today's business while underinvesting in future options.
- Innovation projects exist, but teams lack shared criteria for whether risk is falling and evidence is getting stronger.
- Explore teams are judged by Exploit KPIs and are killed too early.
- Canvases, experiments, and culture tools are disconnected from management decisions.

## Contents / reading map

1. **Business Model Portfolio** — separates Exploit and Explore and shows why a company needs both.
2. **Portfolio Map** — positions businesses by risk and return to guide invest, pivot, transfer, divest, or kill decisions.
3. **Explore** — innovation journeys, evidence building, project stages, and common myths.
4. **Exploit** — how mature businesses improve, defend, and avoid being disrupted.
5. **Innovation Culture** — organization, incentives, leadership, and practices that make exploration possible.
6. **Innovation Metrics** — evidence, risk reduction, and portfolio health instead of only short-term revenue.

## How it maps to PinGarden

- `portfolio-map` — the main entry for comparing core, growth, and exploratory businesses.
- `experiment-canvas` — turns Explore hypotheses into evidence-building paths.
- `innovation-culture-map` — checks whether the organization lets exploration survive.
- `business-model-canvas` — each portfolio item should still resolve to a clear business-model logic.

## What to read first

- For **corporate innovation portfolio work**, start with Business Model Portfolio and Portfolio Map.
- For **innovation review meetings**, read Explore and Innovation Metrics; ask how much risk has been reduced.
- For **organization design**, read Innovation Culture and look for time, budget, authority, and protection mechanisms.
- For **mature-business transformation**, read Exploit to see how existing models improve and defend.

## Common misreadings

- **Portfolio Map is not just a project board.** It is a resource-allocation and exit-decision tool.
- **Explore projects should not be judged by immediate ROI only.** Early evidence and risk reduction matter more.
- **Innovation culture is not only creativity.** It must show up in governance, incentives, metrics, and protection.
